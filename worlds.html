Worlds

A page containing every world planned for PepperTux Legacy.

Implemented Worlds

Icy island

This world is a cold, icy place with a lot of snow. It's also where the game starts, as Tux and Penny have a picnic here, only for it to be interrupted by Nolok making Tux fall unconscious to kidnap Penny, so Tux has to save her. At the end, Tux is chased by a Yeti and the Yeti boss fight begins.

Worlds In Development

Forest World

This world is a warm forest with lots of trees. It's where the Granitos live, although the forest is being corrupted by Nolok's magic spreading throughout the forest, turning it into a Ghost Forest. At what's almost the end, Tux has to run towards the Ghost Tree, a being that's spreading dangerous roots across the Ghost Forest. Tux does this to press a button put there by Nolok, which is different because it's green and it destroys the Ghost Tree. The end is just a light forest level that's visibly damaged.

Worlds Planned But Not In Development

Snow Mountain

This world is a very cold snowy place that takes place on a mountain. Tux first arrives by crashing a helicopter on the side of the mountain since he's not good at that stuff but is somehow mostly unharmed from it and continues anyways.

When he reaches the peak, he finds Nolok on a metal bridge, saying that he was waiting for Tux and to meet him at his castle. However, the castle's entrance needs five keys to open it and those five keys are on the mountain's peak. They spell out the word "NOLOK".

The key levels would be a forest, a cave of lava, a crystal cave, an ocean on the mountain and a level where you go up to the clouds. Eventually, Tux would find all keys and unlock the castle, which would contain a bomb factory, a room of lava that creates the cave of lava and finally, Nolok's factory, which he'd control and attempt to defeat Tux using the factory's machines.

Nolok would fail at using his factory and his final plan would be to use roots that look like the Ghost Tree's roots, while Nolok would act like the Yeti as he doesn't have good fighting skills. Nolok would be defeated, Penny would be saved, credits would play, nothing interesting afterwards as the story is over.

Proposed Worlds

Tropical Island

A possible World 3

This world is a warm beach with a jungle at the center of it. It's where the Master Totem lives. It spreads evil Totems far across the Tropical Island, along with flames. The Master Totem also makes the normal tropical creatures like the toads evil. Tux has to make his way to the center of the island to get to the jungle, then to the end of the jungle to find a temple with the Master Totem in it, who agrees to explode if Tux can solve his platforming challenges and press three buttons. Tux presses all three buttons, the Master Totem explodes, the Master Totem's evil curse on the Tropical Island creatures fades away and Tux goes through one more level. He can also find a secret exit in the jungle that leads him to Secret Desert / Desert Island.

Secret Desert / Desert Island

Secret Desert is if it's not World 3, Desert Island is if it's World 3.

This world is a very hot desert with a pyramid at the end of it. Unlike all other worlds, Nolok doesn't believe Tux would survive the desert, so he forgets to place a boss at the end (If it's World 3, this changes to Nolok placing Master Totem before Tux arrives and Master Totme's curse creates evil creatures) and Tux gets through anyways, despite the desert's heat and everything that's in his way. The pyramid at the end needs to be unlocked with three crystals from Icy Island, Forest World and this world. When Tux gets through, he finds a helicopter and crashes it on the side of Snow Mountain and although confused about what just happened, he's not badly harmed from it (he doesn't even lose his powerup!) and has to get through Snow Mountain anyways.
