Ideas

These are ideas that may or may not end up being added to the mod. If you have an at-least-almost-decent idea, tell her to add it here. Even rejected ideas will end up here until the mod is finished unless it can't be implemented (legal issue (such as the usage of logos) or too hard to add). If you don't want your name on your idea on this page, also tell her.

Various ideas by Michael Maiky originally intended for SuperTux but became ideas for PepperTux Legacy

Tux going fast like Sonic

This is planned to be done using things that push Tux forwards in levels, like conveyor belts and the sideways trampoline things. It's also already kind of implemented with sliding.

Snake Enemy

This has been planned for a while for the proposed Tropical Island world but may be done for the Ghost Forest if Tropical Island is scrapped. It won't go on walls, unfortunately.

Tutorial Level

Has already been (sort of) implemented with the level "Welcome To PepperTux" being a very easy level, but there is an idea to turn the Icy Island beginning cutscene into a "tutorial" (just a level that's very easy) level that ends in the cutscene.

Various ideas by Vaesea

The Add-On Repository

It currently serves no purpose, but it shouldn't be removed. It would be good to remove the already-existing add-on to add a new one and add the add-on repository stuff to the mod. Also, related idea, there should be an add-on that's like a harder version of around 10 levels from PepperTux's story that's included in PepperTux to provide a reward for getting the Tux Dolls.

Replace Tropical Island with Heated Desert

This shouldn't just be a clone of SuperTux (but with 0.5.1 stuff), it should have some unique things too. There's already sprites for desert enemies, and 5 worlds is definitely too much. Tropical Island should be made into an add-on and Heated Desert should be World 3.

Crystal in Quick Level?

There's a crystal in Quick Level that doesn't serve a purpose. It should unlock a pyramid in Heated Desert, possibly needing 2 more crystals from Forest World and Heated Desert itself.

Shops

Shops would be cool to add, as the coins serve no purpose right now. Maybe the Walrus Salesman concept could be used?

All workflows (OS-related) from SuperTux

Just so all two SuperTux fans who also use FreeBSD can play PepperTux Legacy. Also the five SuperTux fans who use Mac OS would be able to play PepperTux Legacy. Most use Windows or Linux but it'd be a good idea anyways.

Replace beginning cutscene of Icy Island with tutorial

This would be good for those who somehow haven't played SuperTux or hasn't played it in a long time and decides to play PepperTux.
